Creative Coding? (p1)

Creative coding is a kind of ambiguous term. It is an expression of the balance between mechanical and organic, order and chaos.

Creative coding appeals to all audiences.

  • A good way to begin programming
  • A good way to get a fresh perspective on programming

Languages

p5.js and Processing (Java) are frequently chosen for their accessibility and high levels of abstraction.

Other libraries can help with sketches that require more advanced math, like Matter.js for physics.

However, making generative systems does not require advanced math or programming skills.
